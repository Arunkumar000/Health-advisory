Suggested Medications Based on Your Diagnosis

Medication Name: Ibuprofen

Description: Ibuprofen is a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to reduce fever, pain, and inflammation.

Usage: Take as prescribed by your doctor. Commonly used for headaches, muscle aches, or minor injuries.

Side Effects: Upset stomach, dizziness, or gastrointestinal issues. Always consult your doctor before use.

Medication Name: Paracetamol

Description: Paracetamol (acetaminophen) is used to treat mild to moderate pain and reduce fever.

Usage: Recommended dosage depends on your age and condition. Used for pain relief and fever control.

Side Effects: Can cause liver damage in high doses. Consult your doctor before use if you have liver conditions.

Medication Name: Antibiotics (Amoxicillin)

Description: Amoxicillin is a broad-spectrum antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections.

Usage: Follow the prescribed dosage. Do not stop taking early, even if symptoms improve.

Side Effects: Diarrhea, nausea, or allergic reactions. Contact your healthcare provider for concerns.
